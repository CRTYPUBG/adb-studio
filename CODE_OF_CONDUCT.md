# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation harassment-free regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity or orientation.

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, focus on community benefit, and avoid harassment,
threats, discrimination, doxxing or sexualized conduct. Maintainers may remove content, restrict
participation and report credible threats. Report conduct issues privately through the repository
owner. Enforcement decisions will be documented confidentially and applied consistently.

This policy follows Contributor Covenant 2.1: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/
